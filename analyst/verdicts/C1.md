# C1 — Soura ↔ Lal Chowk (old-city spine)  ·  MATCHED → FDR-050

**Support:** 73 runs · 17 drivers · 42 days · 13.2 km · one-way 63.5 min ·
moving 20.0 km/h · effective 12.5 km/h · operating 07:00–20:00 IST (peak 14:00).

## Verdict
- **O→D:** Soura (NE Srinagar, SKIMS area) ↔ Lal Mandi / Lal Chowk (central),
  **via the old city** (Nowhatta – Gojwara). North–south city spine.
- **Match:** **FDR-050 "Soura to Lalchowk"** — *high confidence*.
- **Informal:** no. **Plausible:** yes (busiest corridor in the whole dataset).

## Why not the higher-overlap candidates
Geometry overlap ranks these top:
| candidate | overlap | why it's wrong |
|---|---|---|
| Srinagar → Ganderbal (FDR-288) | 0.73 | continues **north past Soura to Ganderbal**; observed line stops at Soura |
| Rangpora → Dalgate (FDR-105) | 0.73 | shares the central trunk only; different termini |
| Srinagar → Buchpora (FDR-052) | 0.73 | shares trunk; Buchpora branch not the observed O→D |
| **Soura → Lalchowk (FDR-050)** | **0.71** | **endpoints match exactly** ✅ |

A pure overlap threshold would mis-assign this to Srinagar–Ganderbal. The map
shows the observed corridor terminating at Soura (not continuing to Ganderbal),
and the endpoints geocode to Soura and Lal Mandi (≈ Lal Chowk) — so FDR-050 is
correct despite being the 0.71, not the 0.73.

## Web grounding

Munawarabad, Nowhata, Gojwara, Hawal, Alamgari Bazar, Nowshera, Soura** — the
same corridor (TRC adjoins Lal Chowk/Lal Mandi). Confirms a real, major route.
